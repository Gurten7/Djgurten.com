Biography

Behind the decks of Europe’s hottest clubs and the madness of KDW stands one name: DJ Gûrten. Known for his genre-defying sets, energetic drops and charismatic presence, Gûrten has emerged as a driving force in the Dutch electronic music scene.

With roots in both beach beats and underground vibes, DJ Gûrten blends tradition with experimentation. He first broke through during the legendary “Litac Sessions” and quickly captured the attention of dancefloors from Belgrade to Berkhout.

In 2026 he takes it up a notch with his first international headline tour: Colors of Sound. With a mix of EDM, brass stabs, Dutch rap and emotional buildups, his new sound is made for both festival fields and neon-lit clubs.

Whether it's Ushuaïa at sunset, a mystery set at KDW '26, or a pop-up party in a parking garage, DJ Gûrten delivers. Every. Single. Time.
