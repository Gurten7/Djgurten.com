Electric Groove – The Album

Released in March 2025, Electric Groove is the debut album from DJ Gûrten — a sonic journey blending futuristic funk, velvet house, retro electro and peak-time EDM. Featuring standout singles like Electric Groove, Velvet Circuit and Neon Moves, the album is both a tribute to classic club energy and a bold step into new soundscapes.
