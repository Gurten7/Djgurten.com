Electric Groove

Electric Groove is DJ Gûrten’s most ambitious project to date – blending nu disco, electronic funk, brass elements and experimental house into a dazzling sonic ride.

Each track tells its own story while together forming a nonstop energy wave. Perfect for late nights, summer drives, or festival flashbacks.
