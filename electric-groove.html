Electric Groove

The Album

Electric Groove is DJ Gûrten’s genre-bending debut album, blending electronic funk, retro disco, deep house, and brass-infused madness. From underground club vibes to festival bangers, this album captures the full range of Gûrten’s wild sonic identity.

Including standout tracks like Velvet Circuit, Neon Moves and the title track Electric Groove, the album delivers high voltage energy and rich textures.
